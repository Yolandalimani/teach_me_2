About us

BACKGROUND

The company was founded in 2020 to address the challenges faced in mainstream school system such as the lack
of personalised attention, limited opportunities for self-paced learning to improve and equip the necessary skills
needed to excel in their studies.

OUR MISSION

We are on a mission to avail learning opportunities beyond the standard physical classes to provide learners with
high quality e-learning that is flexible and accessible for them while supplementing the mainstream system.
